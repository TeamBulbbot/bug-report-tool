Before we start
  • Make sure you're reliably able to reproduce the issue. If you don't have reliable reproduction steps please contact support instead
  • Make sure to check the relevant Discord Bugs board to see if your bug hasn't been already reported.

  • Desktop Bugs: https://bugs.discord.com/project/view/3/
  • Linux Bugs: https://bugs.discord.com/project/view/18/
  • Overlay Bugs: https://bugs.discord.com/project/view/15/
  • Android Bugs: https://bugs.discord.com/project/view/1/
  • iOS Bugs: https://bugs.discord.com/project/view/2/
  • Marketing Bugs: https://bugs.discord.com/project/view/14/


  • If you didn't find it to be reported on Discord Bugs you can also use the relevent report channel and use the search command inside of Discord.
  • Please note that this site will NOT submit the bug for you.
  • If you want to attach an image/video to your report please ask a Bug Squasher (or above) or a Jr. Mod to attach it for you

Windows
How do you get your Windows Version
  1. Press Windows Key + r
  2. Write winver
  3. Press Enter
  4. On the second line you will find your Windows Version.

How do you get your Discord Version
  1. Open the Discord Client
  2. Open up the User settings
  3. Look at the bottom left
  4. Right below the social media links and the log out button you will find your Discord version

How do you get your Discord Version #2
  1. Open the Discord Client and press Control + r
  2. Press Control + Shift + i
  3. Now look for the Filter field at the top of the console
  4. Inside of that write Build

Got it
Mac
How do you get your Mac Version
  1. Choose Apple menu
  2. About This Mac

How do you get your Discord Version
  1. Open the Discord Client
  2. Open up the User settings
  3. Look at the bottom left
  4. Right below the social media links and the log out button you will find your Discord version

How do you get your Discord Version #2

  4. Inside of that write Build

Android
How do you get your Android Version
  1. Open the setting application
  2. Scroll down until you see either About Phone or About Device and tap it
  3. Tap Software Info

How do you get your Discord Version
  1. Open the Discord Client
  2. Go to the User Settings and scroll down
  3. Almost at the bottom you will find App Information

Got it
IOS
How do you get your iPhone Version
  1. Open the settings app
  2. Press General
  3. Press About
  4. Press Software information

How do you get your Discord Version
  1. Open the Discord Client
  2. Go to the User Settings and scroll down
  3. For TestFlight users open up the TestFlight application

Got it
Browser
How do you get your Browser Version
Firefox
  1. Click the menu icon
  2. Click Help
  3. Select About Firefox

Chrome
  1. Click the menu icon (Top right of the screen)
  2. Then press Help
  3. Then tap About Chrome

Safari
  1. Click the Safari icon
  2. In the menu bar, click Safari
  3. Then choice About Safari

Edge
  1. Click the menu icon (Top right of the the screen)
  2. Press Settings
  3. Scroll down

How do you get your Discord Version
  1. Go to Discord
  2. Refresh the website
  3. Open up the developer console
  4. Chrome (Windows): Control + Shift + J, Chrome (Mac): Control + Option + J
  5. Firefox (Windows): Control + Shift + K, Chrome (Mac): Control + Option + K
  6. Edge: F12
  7. Safari: Inside of the Develop tab, press Show JavaScript Console (You will need to enable developer mode for this)
  8. Inside of that write Build

Got it
